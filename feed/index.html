As we navigate through a rapidly changing world, our approach to real estate development at GMH has fortunately had to evolve. As part of this evolution, we’ve seen the rise of eco-conscious development, a transformation that has required us to adapt and innovate. Today, I’d like to share our journey towards becoming a key player in this field and how we, at GMH, have incorporated this approach into our core values. Facing realities such as climate change, environmental degradation and resource scarcity, we recognized the urgency of adopting more sustainable practices in our industry. It was clear to us that it wasn’t just about designing and constructing buildings anymore. We had a larger role to play in preserving our natural resources, and providing healthier living spaces for our clients.

Taking these insights to heart, we’ve aimed to align GMH with the emerging trends of eco-conscious development. Our latest venture, KAIA, is the embodiment of this commitment, representing our vision for sustainable and luxurious living. KAIA isn’t just about eco-friendly homes. It represents a comprehensive approach to sustainable community development. We’ve designed the project to maximise natural light and air flow, reducing the need for artificial cooling and heating. We’ve also introduced green roofs and gardens to boost air quality and decrease stormwater runoff, all while providing residents with access to nature.

Each element of KAIA, from the villas to the courtyard houses and duplexes, strikes a balance between luxury, functionality, and sustainability. With high ceilings, useful mezzanines, and ample outdoor spaces, we’re demonstrating that design can accommodate environmental goals without compromising on comfort or aesthetics. Beyond the individual homes, we’re cultivating a sense of community within KAIA. We’re promoting activities that encourage sustainable living, such as shared social spaces and community gardens, aligning enjoyable pastimes with positive environmental impact.

Ability to Transform Ideas into Reality

At GMH, our dedication to eco-conscious development extends beyond KAIA and permeates everything we do. We continually strive to improve, aiming to meet and exceed international standards in sustainability. It’s part of our mission to deliver sustainable, high-quality residences that exceed our customer’s expectations. In 2021, we established 3H Construction to further our commitment to sustainable building practices. This step has been crucial in enhancing our technical operations, allowing us to focus on creating more sustainable buildings across various market segments.

We believe our journey mirrors the wider trend within the real estate industry. By prioritising eco-conscious development, we aim to help shape a future where construction development contributes actively to the creation of sustainable, vibrant communities. As this transformation continues to unfold, we are excited to continue to lead the way, creating luxury homes that satisfy, and even surpass, the expectations of eco-conscious living.

In the evolving landscape of residential living, we, at GMH Development, have proudly embarked on our latest venture, KAIA. As a North-Cyprus based company, we are fusing architectural elegance and sustainable living to redefine community-based living. KAIA, our new coastal haven, merges seamlessly with the serenity of its natural habitat, offering an idyllic and sustainable residential experience. Nestled amidst the picturesque landscape, we’ve crafted eight exclusive villas. With a spacious 145 sqm of net interior, lofty ceilings, a useful mezzanine, and generous outdoor spaces, each villa is a testament to our unwavering commitment to design and quality. Here, luxury coexists with sustainability, offering residents an eco-friendly haven with unrivalled sea views.

Just a stone’s throw from these villas, our Courtyard houses enrich the diversity of the KAIA community. Each 145 sqm unit, complete with a 3+1 loft and a separate 1+1 guest house, encapsulates our understanding of contemporary living. From the versatile mezzanine to the terraces and gardens, we’ve designed these houses to adapt to the varying lifestyles of our residents. In addition to our courtyards, our unique two-bedroom duplex houses offer an intimate retreat. With a 95 sqm interior space, these units are designed to emphasise relaxation and privacy. From a functional mezzanine and office to the possibility of a jacuzzi on the balcony of the third-floor master bedroom, we’re redefining luxury living.  And last but not least, our attached one and two-bedroom apartment block offer a more lively and integrated community experience as they are located right by the pool as well as the amenities building.

In Perfect Balance With Nature

Our roots reach deep into the 30-year legacy of Mesan Development and Mesan Construction, from where we draw our commitment to creating contemporary, luxurious, and sustainable residences. The 2021 launch of our sister company, 3H Construction, has reinforced our dedication to superior construction services and technical operations. Together, we bring to the table three decades of experience and a portfolio encompassing nearly 450,000 square metres of completed and ongoing projects. This vast experience embodies our customer-centric approach and unwavering dedication to excellence.

But KAIA is more than just a collection of residences. It’s a fully integrated community designed to attract a broad spectrum of residents, from young professionals to families. We have created a lifestyle that emphasises wellness, collaboration, and community spirit while offering a host of amenities like social spaces, wellness areas, and a community garden.

KAIA exemplifies our vision at GMH Development: to innovate and push the boundaries of residential living. We are not only meeting but surpassing customer expectations with the marriage of design, quality, and a unique community living concept. With KAIA, we continue to champion a life that is in perfect balance with nature.
