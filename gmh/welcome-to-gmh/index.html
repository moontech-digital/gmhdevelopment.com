Welcome to renewed GMH Development

Welcome to GMH Development, a leading construction and development company based in North Cyprus. Established in 2015, our company has quickly gained a reputation for professionalism and excellence in the industry. With deep roots in Mesan Development and Mesan Construction, which boast thirty years of experience, GMH Development is built on a foundation of expertise and innovation. At GMH Development, we specialise in creating contemporary, luxurious, and sustainable residences. Our commitment to quality and design ensures that our projects meet and exceed global standards in the real estate sector. We continuously strive to improve our offerings, aiming for excellence in every aspect of our work.

In 2021, we expanded our operations by establishing 3H Construction as a sister company. This new venture enables us to provide comprehensive construction services, taking technical operations to the next level. With 3H Construction, we undertake a wide range of projects across diverse market segments, including more complex undertakings such as schools and commercial buildings. With a combined experience of thirty years and a portfolio encompassing nearly 450,000 square metres of completed and ongoing projects, GMH Development is poised for continued growth in the industry. We are dedicated to prioritising customer satisfaction above all else. Our goal is not just to meet expectations, but to exceed them. To achieve this, we assemble experienced teams that embody the values of science, honesty, cooperation, and hard work.

Striking for Excellence

At GMH Development, we believe that every project is an opportunity to create something exceptional. We take pride in our ability to transform ideas into reality, delivering outstanding results that leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re looking for a dream home or a commercial space that stands out from the crowd, we are here to turn your vision into a masterpiece. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can assist you in achieving your construction and development goals.
